Round 1 Result

Team CSI presents the much-awaited results of round 1 (Synopsis + Optional Tasks).
We congratulate each and every team who have qualified for the presentation round.

Note

All the qualifying teams have to submit their presentations by 4th March,2017 (9:00am) at csi@outlook.in. In case any team fails to submit their presentation within the given duration of time, the team will get disqualified.

Concept of Waiting List

For all those whose team ID's have been mentioned in the waiting list, they will also have to submit their presentation by 4th March,2017 (9:00am) at csi@outlook.in. In case any of the qualifying teams fails to submit their presentation within time then a chance would be given to those teams who are in the waiting list and have submitted their presentation within the deadline.

  • Points to remember while making presentation
    1. You must mail your presentation by March 4,17 till 9:00am.
    2. All participants should be dressed in formals.
    3. Duration of the presentation must be 8 minutes approx.
    4. Questionnaire will be of 3 minutes.
    5. The presentation must be solution oriented.
